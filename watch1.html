Synopsis

Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy, returns home to find his family slaughtered by a demon. His sister Nezuko is the only survivor, but she has been turned into a demon herself. To restore her humanity and avenge his family, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps. Together with his fierce companions, he embarks on a perilous journey through shadows, swords, and unbreakable bonds.

The series redefined modern anime with breathtaking animation by Ufotable, emotional depth, and legendary fight sequences. Winner of numerous awards including Anime of the Year.

Details & Credits

Demon Slayer (Kimetsu no Yaiba) adapts the hit manga by Koyoharu Gotouge. Produced by Ufotable, the series became a global phenomenon with its stunning visual effects and emotional storytelling. The first season aired in 2019, followed by the Mugen Train arc and subsequent seasons.

Experience the breathtaking journey of Tanjiro and Nezuko as they face the Twelve Kizuki and the terrifying Muzan Kibutsuji.
