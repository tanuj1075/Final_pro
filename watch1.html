Detailed Information

Full Description

Mitsuha Miyamizu is a high school girl living in the rural town of Itomori. She is bored with her life and wishes to be a handsome boy living in Tokyo. Taki Tachibana is a high school boy living in Tokyo who works part-time at an Italian restaurant and aspires to become an architect or an artist.

One day, Mitsuha and Taki suddenly swap bodies. They experience each other's lives, communicate by leaving notes, and eventually develop feelings for each other. But when they try to meet, they discover a tragic secret that transcends time and space.

Your Name is a masterpiece that combines breathtaking animation with a deeply emotional story about love, fate, and memory.
