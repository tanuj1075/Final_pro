Synopsis

Mitsuha Miyamizu is a high school girl living in the rural town of Itomori. She longs for the bustling life of Tokyo. Taki Tachibana is a high school boy in Tokyo who works part-time at an Italian restaurant and aspires to become an architect or an artist.

One day, Mitsuha and Taki suddenly swap bodies. They begin to live each other's lives, leaving notes and setting rules for each other. As they experience life through each other's eyes, they develop a connection. But a catastrophe threatens to sever that connection forever.

The manga adaptation beautifully captures the emotional depth and stunning visuals of the original film, expanding on character thoughts and adding new details to this timeless story of love, fate, and memory.

About the Creators

Makoto Shinkai

Original Creator

Makoto Shinkai

Director, Writer, Original Creator

Makoto Shinkai is a Japanese filmmaker, manga artist, and novelist. He is best known for directing Your Name (2016), which became the highest-grossing anime film worldwide at the time of its release. Shinkai is celebrated for his visually stunning works and emotional storytelling that often explores themes of distance, love, and time.

Other notable works include 5 Centimeters per Second (2007), The Garden of Words (2013), and Weathering with You (2019).

Ranmaru Kotone

Manga Artist

Ranmaru Kotone

Manga Adaptation Artist

Ranmaru Kotone is a Japanese manga artist known for the manga adaptation of Your Name. Kotone skillfully adapted Shinkai's film into manga form, capturing the emotional depth and visual beauty of the original while adding new perspectives and details.

The manga adaptation was serialized in Kodansha's Monthly Afternoon magazine from June 2016 to August 2017 and was collected in three volumes.
